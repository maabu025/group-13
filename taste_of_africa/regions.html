Culinary Regions of Africa

West Africa

Known for bold flavors and communal eating, West African cuisine uses staples like cassava, plantains, and palm oil. Popular dishes include Jollof rice and Egusi soup.

Try Jollof Rice »

East Africa

East African cuisine includes dishes like Ugali and Injera, often accompanied by stews. The use of spices like cardamom and coconut milk reflect Indian and Arabic influence.

Try Ugali »

Southern Africa

Southern African food features a fusion of indigenous and colonial cooking. Dishes like Bobotie reflect Cape Malay influence, blending spices and sweetness.
