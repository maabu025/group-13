Jollof Rice

Jollof Rice is a beloved West African dish with rich tomato and pepper flavors. It's a symbol of celebration, commonly enjoyed at parties and gatherings. Originating from the Senegambian region, it has evolved across various countries like Nigeria and Ghana.

Cooking Instructions

Start by blending tomatoes, red peppers, and onions. Saut√© onions and garlic, then add tomato paste and blended mixture. Season and add parboiled rice. Simmer until rice is tender.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups parboiled rice
  • 1 can tomato paste
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 onion
  • Garlic and ginger
  • Vegetable oil
  • Spices (thyme, curry, bouillon)
