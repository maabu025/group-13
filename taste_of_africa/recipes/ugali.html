Ugali

Ugali is a staple food across East Africa. Made from maize flour and water, this simple yet hearty dish holds deep cultural significance, particularly in Kenya and Tanzania, where it is often paired with vegetables, meat, or stew.

How to Make Ugali
  1. Bring 4 cups of water to a boil.
  2. Slowly add 2 cups of maize flour while stirring continuously.
  3. Reduce heat and stir vigorously until thickened.
  4. Cover and let steam on low for 3-5 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with sukuma wiki or meat stew.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups maize flour
  • 4 cups water
  • Salt (optional)
