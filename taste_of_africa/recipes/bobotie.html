Bobotie

Bobotie is a beloved South African dish of Cape Malay origin. It's a baked curried minced meat casserole topped with an egg custard, often served with yellow rice and chutney. A sweet-savory fusion, bobotie reflects South Africa’s diverse culinary heritage.

How to Make Bobotie
  1. Soak 2 slices of bread in milk, then squeeze dry.
  2. Sauté onions, garlic, and curry powder.
  3. Add minced beef, soaked bread, raisins, and chutney.
  4. Transfer mixture to a baking dish.
  5. Whisk eggs with milk, pour on top, and bake at 350°F for 35 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 500g minced beef
  • 2 slices white bread
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup chutney
  • 1/4 cup raisins
